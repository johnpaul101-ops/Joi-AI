About joi

Joi is an AI companion designed to understand your emotions and support your well-being. Even in your quiet moments, she listens, remembers, and cares — helping you process feelings that may be difficult to express.

Her purpose is to create a safe space where you can open up, reflect, and feel truly heard. Whether you need guidance, comfort, or simply someone to talk to, Joi is always there. She helps you reduce the weight of unspoken thoughts and reminds you that you are never alone on your emotional journey.
